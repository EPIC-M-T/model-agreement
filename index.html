EPIC Models & Talent LLC
Model Agreement – EPIC Casablanca Shoot

This Agreement is entered into by and between EPIC Models & Talent LLC, a Nevada limited liability company (“Company”), and the undersigned individual (“Model”).

1. Shoot Information

Project Name: EPIC Casablanca Shoot

Shoot Date: Tuesday, June 9

Primary Location:
Casablanca
2818 E. Quail Ave
Las Vegas, NV 89120

Secondary Locations:
Additional shooting locations may include a gym, Pilates studio, yoga studio, or other fitness, wellness, or lifestyle venues as agreed upon by the Model and Company.

2. Scope of Work

Model agrees to participate in photo and/or video content creation for the EPIC Casablanca Shoot as directed by the Company.

The total contracted work period shall be up to eight (8) hours, anticipated as follows:

  • Approximately six (6) hours at Casablanca
  • Approximately two (2) hours at an additional fitness or wellness location

Actual production timing, locations, and scheduling may vary based upon creative, production, weather, operational requirements, or venue availability.

3. Compensation

Model shall be compensated at the previously agreed-upon day rate for participation in the EPIC Casablanca Shoot.

Model compensation shall be paid upon the Model’s arrival at the designated shoot location. Compensation covers the agreed booking period and all participation related to the shoot.

Any extension of the booking period beyond the agreed schedule must be approved by the Company.

4. Age & Legal Eligibility

Given the nature of the wardrobe requirements, the Model represents and warrants that they are at least eighteen (18) years of age and possess the legal authority to enter into this Agreement.

The Model agrees to present a valid, government-issued photo ID upon arrival at the shoot to verify their age and identity.

5. Meals & Transportation

Meals & Drinks: The Company will provide a minimum of one (1) meal and drinks throughout the day.

Transportation: The Model is responsible for their own transportation to the primary Casablanca location, as well as transportation between the primary location and any secondary fitness or wellness locations.

6. Wardrobe, Hair & Makeup

Hair and makeup services may be provided by the Company as needed.

The Model agrees to:

  • Arrive at the designated call time fully prepared and ready to shoot.
  • Arrive in appropriate wardrobe with hair and makeup completed unless otherwise instructed.
  • Maintain a French manicure and French pedicure suitable for photography and video production.

The following wardrobe items will be provided by the Company:

  • Lingerie
  • Swimwear

The Model agrees to bring the following items if available:

  • One pair of black pumps
  • One pair of nude pumps
  • Additional heels, wedges, or footwear options

The Model is encouraged to bring additional wardrobe and styling options, including but not limited to:

  • Jewelry
  • Additional swimwear
  • Gowns
  • Dresses
  • Fitness apparel

7. Liability & Personal Property

The Model acknowledges that they are voluntarily participating in this shoot, which may include fitness or lifestyle activities.

The Model assumes all risks associated with participation and holds the Company harmless for any personal injury, except to the extent caused by the Company’s gross negligence or willful misconduct.

The Company is not responsible for any loss, theft, or damage to the Model’s personal wardrobe, jewelry, equipment, phone, bags, or belongings brought to the set.

8. Professional Conduct

Model agrees to conduct themselves professionally throughout the duration of the production and to follow reasonable creative direction provided by the Company, photographer, videographer, and production staff.

The Company is committed to maintaining a positive, respectful, and collaborative working environment.

Any behavior deemed disruptive, hostile, unprofessional, disrespectful, unsafe, or unsupportive of fellow participants, staff, clients, or venue personnel may result in the Model being immediately removed from the shoot at the sole discretion of the Company.

9. Independent Contractor Status

Model acknowledges and agrees that they are participating as an independent contractor and not as an employee, partner, joint venturer, or representative of EPIC Models & Talent LLC.

The Model shall be solely responsible for any applicable federal, state, and local taxes or obligations arising from compensation received under this Agreement.

10. Social Media & Content Sharing

Model agrees to the following social media requirements when posting any approved content from the EPIC Casablanca Shoot:

1. Tagging & Credit

Model agrees to tag or mention EPIC Models & Talent, PB3 Productions, Casablanca Las Vegas, and any other required campaign partners when posting approved photos, videos, reels, stories, or other content from the shoot.

2. Proper Disclosure

If the post is sponsored, paid, promotional, gifted, or otherwise connected to compensation or business promotion, Model agrees to use clear disclosure such as “#ad,” “#sponsored,” “Paid partnership,” or any platform-required disclosure tool when applicable.

3. Share-Friendly Campaign Support

Model agrees to use reasonable, authentic calls to action when appropriate, such as asking followers to save, share, tag a friend, or send the content to someone who may be interested.

Model shall not use spam, bots, fake engagement, mass unsolicited direct messages, misleading tactics, engagement pods, purchased followers, purchased views, or artificial engagement methods.

4. Usage & Post Visibility

Model agrees not to delete, hide, remove tags, or materially alter approved campaign-related posts.

Model also grants the Company permission to repost, share, embed, display, and use approved campaign-related social media content for marketing, website, portfolio, advertising, social media, and promotional purposes.

Failure to provide appropriate credit or follow approved posting requirements may result in the removal of content and/or affect future booking opportunities.

11. Set Confidentiality & Personal Recording Policy

To protect the privacy of participants, the creative process, and proprietary content, no Model shall capture, record, livestream, photograph, or distribute any behind-the-scenes footage, images, audio recordings, or other content from the shoot without prior written or verbal authorization from the Company.

The use of personal cell phones, cameras, or recording devices for content creation on set is strictly prohibited unless expressly approved.

Unauthorized recording, photography, videography, livestreaming, or distribution of content may result in:

  • Immediate removal from the production
  • Forfeiture of any unpaid compensation
  • Loss of access to final images and video content
  • Termination of future booking opportunities

The Company reserves the right to require the immediate removal of any unauthorized content posted online.

The Model acknowledges that all content created during the production is the exclusive property of the Company, PB3 Productions, Casablanca Las Vegas, and/or their authorized partners.

Any approved behind-the-scenes recording or personal social media content shall remain subject to the Company’s posting instructions, approval rights, tagging requirements, disclosure requirements, confidentiality rules, brand safety standards, and social media requirements set forth in this Agreement.

Model Cancellation

If the Model cancels within twenty-four (24) hours of the scheduled shoot time or fails to appear, the Company reserves the right to terminate future booking and representation opportunities at its sole discretion, and no compensation will be owed.

Company Cancellation / Weather

The Company reserves the right to postpone or cancel the shoot due to inclement weather, venue unavailability, acts of God, emergency, illness, safety concerns, operational restrictions, or other unforeseen circumstances.

If the Company cancels the shoot prior to the Model’s call time, no compensation shall be owed to the Model, and all parties will work in good faith to reschedule.
